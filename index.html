Frequently Asked Questions

How far are you from the cruise terminal?

We’re only a few blocks from the terminals. Due to port rules, we cannot enter port property. We’ll drop you as close as possible:
• For ships at 25th St & Harborside Dr — about 0.22 miles (less than 5‑minute walk).
• For ships opposite New Strand — directly across the street from Park & Wash.

What security measures do you have?

24/7 security cameras, perimeter fencing, lighting, and staff on‑site during business hours.

When should I arrive for my cruise?

Arrive at least 1.5 hours before your boarding time to allow for parking and the short walk from our drop‑off point.

Can I modify or cancel my reservation?

Yes — modifications/cancellations are accepted up to 24 hours before your scheduled parking date.
